FEATURES OF CENTRIFUGAL MONOBLOCK PUMPS :
  • Powerful Motor to withstand wide voltage fluctuations between 180V - 240V & 360V - 415V
  • Motor Winding fitted with thermal Overload Protector to avoid coil burning due to unrecommended application of the pump.
  • Double Shielded high quality ball bearing lubricated for life to avoid periodic maintanance and ensure long life.
  • Totally Enclosed Fan Cooled (TEFC) type motor design for better protection.
  • High quality mechanical seal.
  • Class "B" Insulation designed for continuous duty.

APPLICATIONS OF CENTRIFUGAL MONOBLOCK PUMPS :
  • Domestic water pumping to overhead tanks.
  • Fountains.
  • Industrial applications.
  • Curring construction sites.
  • Gardening sprinkling of lawns.
